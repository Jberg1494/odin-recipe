Thai Coconut Soup

This Thai coconut soup is full of authentic, bold, and delicious Thai flavors. This is the best Thai soup recipe out there â€” you won't be disappointed with this one! Serve over steamed rice.

Ingredients

    1 tablespoon vegetable oil

    2 tablespoons of grates fresh ginger
    2 teaspoons red curry paste
    1 stalk lemongrass
    4 cups chicken broth
    3 tablespoons fish sauce
    1 tablespoon light brown sugar
    3 cans of coconut milk
    1/2 pound of fresh shitake mushrooms
    1 pound medium shrimp - peeled & devained
    2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
    Salt to taste
    1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

    Step 1
    Heat oil in alarge pot over medium head. Add ginger, curry paste and
    lemongrass;cook and stir in the hot oil for 1 minute.
    Step 2
    Gradually stir in chicken broth, then stir in fish sauce and brown sugar; reduce
    heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes.
    Step 3
    Add coconut milk and mushrooms;cook and stir until mushrooms are soft,
    about 5 minutes.
    Step 4
    Add shrimp; cook until no longer translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in lime juice;
    season with salt and garnish with cilantro.
